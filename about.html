The iPhone 7's exterior is similar in shape and volume to iPhone 6 and iPhone 6S. Alongside the existing silver, gold, and rose gold colors, the device is offered in new matte black and "jet black" colors.The "jet black" color is a dark shade, high-gloss black finish.
